About us

We are simply 4 teens, named Guy Porat, Omer Horovitz, Tom Markovich and Or talit. We participated in a course provided by "Ness Technologies", and during this course we had an idea for product that enhances the Virtual Reality experience by adding mid-air haptic feedback to it. With the help of Ness, we worked hard to create this. We researched the need for it and we wrote down every necessary detail needed to get this product underway. Now this simple thought, to add mid-air haptic feedback to VR is closer than ever before to becoming a reality.
